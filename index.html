by HUANG Rui - February 20,2026

WHAT'S NEW--Explore new places every single day

In 2020, my favorite blogger posted a video documenting the process of making his dream come true. In the comments, people shared their ambitions and encouraged one another. I felt the media’s power. Since then, my budding interests in story-telling and communication blossomed. My first attempt in this field was as an Honor’s photographer. I never felt more accomplished than when I created a video that matched my aesthetics and ideals. I was more committed to persuing in this profession.



Find Rest for Your Soul

In 2021, busy schoolwork prevented me from pursuing my interest. I decided to take a year off from school. This year, I took the online Appreciation of Chinese and Foreign Classic Cases of News Photography course, and learned about the categories of photography. Han Li’s lecture made me more innovative in my design. In the 48-hour FilMarathon competition, I was involved in every step of the short film creation process, acquiring expert audio-visual skills and learning how to express my views through my work. During my time as a photographer for promotional videos at two Hainan surf clubs, I found that my desired content was often not what clients needed. To complete tasks, I had to abandon some ideas. That got me thinking: Are the customers’ needs and the creators’ concepts contradictory? My friend and I founded Zizai Studio providing publicity and videography services in 2022. In creating the Live-in-SIDUHE Channel for Siduhe Village’s development project, videos lasted around 5 minutes initially. Regretfully, the view counts were not rosy. We found viewers are now more likely to be attracted by short videos and low-quality dramas. Nonetheless, I believe that disseminators must preserve the works’ artistic integrity and distinctiveness rather than catering to the public blindly. Maybe I can strike a balance between serving my clients’ requirements and keeping my work’s originality.

We decided to take short videos with specific cinematic and editing techniques for better quality and greater artistic value. The Channel had hundreds of thousands of views overall. In a guesthouse promotion program, the customer requested the initial ten seconds quickly grabbed people’s attention. We thought the video should highlight its beauty and encourage viewers to take it slowly. Finally, two promotions were created: one met their requirements and the other had a laid-back, idyllic tone, while representative images were included at the beginning to draw viewers’ attention. After presentation, our idea was approved.

PORTFOLIO (POSTER)

Since joining the German Channel of the Overseas Communication Department at People's Daily Online, I have gradually familiarized myself with the work processes under the careful guidance of my internship supervisor and the channel instructor's. I successfully completed several major projects, including the design of posters for the Sino-German economic and trade series, the production of a n interview video with Continental Tire China Ltd., the production o f a video for the Swiss Embassy event, the creation of German channel short videos for the "I Celebrate Chinese New Year" series, the creation of posters for Chinese New Year customs and countdown events, and the design and production of posters to celebrate the Chinese New Year. Throughout my three-month internship, I approached each task with dedication and seriousness. Under the patient guidance of my mentors, I continuously improved my professional skills. Particularly in areas like topic planning, data verification, and video production, I learned how to identify more impactful topics from the perspective of foreign media, present facts in a rigorous and scientific manner, and inject creativity into the content for foreign public diplomacy.
